About Me

Transforming Ideas into Intelligent Solutions

Hello! I'm Bhagat Pandey, an AI researcher and LLM engineer with a passion for developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions. Currently pursuing my Master's in AI at Kathmandu University, I specialize in Large Language Models, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision.

My journey began with a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering from Tribhuvan University, which provided me with a strong foundation in mathematical modeling and signal processing. This background has proven invaluable in understanding the theoretical underpinnings of modern AI systems.

I'm particularly interested in developing efficient AI solutions for real-world applications, with a focus on multilingual NLP, time series forecasting, and advancing the capabilities of transformer architectures. When I'm not researching, I enjoy teaching others about machine learning and contributing to the AI community.
